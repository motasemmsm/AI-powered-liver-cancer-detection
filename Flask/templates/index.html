Why It Matters

Understanding Liver Health

Know the key facts that can help protect your liver and detect cancer early.

Liver Function

The liver filters toxins, produces essential proteins, and stores vital nutrients for your body's daily functions.

Disease Risk Factors

Hepatitis B, C, alcohol abuse, and obesity significantly increase liver cancer risk. Know your risk factors.

Early Symptoms

Fatigue, abdominal pain, and jaundice are key early signs. Detecting them early improves treatment outcomes.
