How does early liver cancer detection work?

Our system uses advanced AI to analyze CT scan images and identify signs of liver cancer:
  • Upload your CT scan images through our secure platform
  • AI algorithms analyze the images for abnormalities
  • Results are typically available within minutes
  • Our medical team reviews all results for accuracy
Early detection significantly increases the chances of successful treatment.
2

What types of scans can I upload?

We currently accept the following:
  • CT (Computed Tomography) scans
  • Ultrasound images
Supported file formats are JPG and PNG. Please ensure your images are clear and properly oriented.
3

Is my information secure?

Yes, we take data security very seriously. All your medical information is:
  • Encrypted using industry-standard protocols
  • Stored securely on protected servers
  • Never shared with third parties without your consent
  • Compliant with healthcare data protection regulations

How accurate is the detection?

Our AI system is trained on thousands of cases with high accuracy. However:
  • This is a screening tool, not a definitive diagnosis
  • All positive results should be confirmed by a medical professional
  • Results are always reviewed by our certified medical team
Always consult with your doctor for proper medical advice.

How do I create an account?

Creating an account takes less than 2 minutes:
  • Click "Register" in the top navigation
  • Fill in your personal information
  • Create a secure password
  • Agree to the terms of service
Once registered, you can immediately upload scans and receive analysis.
6

What happens after I upload my scan?

After uploading your CT scan:
  • Our AI system analyzes the images immediately
  • Preliminary results are available within minutes
  • A detailed report is generated automatically
  • Our medical team reviews and validates all results
  • Results are available in your profile dashboard
For positive findings, we recommend consulting with a healthcare provider.
